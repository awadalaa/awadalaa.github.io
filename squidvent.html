About

Squidvent's goal is to revolutionize and simplify the event experiences for event organizers by embracing the latest in web and mobile technology.

Founded in 2013 by a web developer working under a major event planning firm, it was apparent that there were no great tools for event organizers. Much of the technology at the time was either out of date or focused solely on the attendee. Squidvent aims to bridge the gap and make it easier to plan events.
